HISTORY

Planetvibes is a locally owned and operated independent bookseller with 7 locations in Sri Lanka. Planetvibes’s origin dates back to the Gold Rush Days of 2000 when Perera struck it in Colombo, set himself up in business selling books. That small bookstore was moved, bought, sold, burned, rebuilt, renamed and became Planetvibes., as we know it today, in 2021
